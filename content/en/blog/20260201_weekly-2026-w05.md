Week of January 26 – February 1. Third straight week dominated by
**[cterm](https://github.com/KarpelesLab/cterm)** — 95 of 100
commits. Features are starting to compound: the terminal you built
last week now has to survive the one you're building this week.

Quiet outside that. Four commits on
**[outscript](https://github.com/KarpelesLab/outscript)** and one on
**[AzusaOS/azusa-opensource-recipes](https://github.com/AzusaOS/azusa-opensource-recipes)**.

No promises this is the last cterm-heavy week, but the shape of the
project is clearer now than when it started three weeks ago.
