Spent a few days in Las Vegas at [Bitcoin 2026](https://2026.b.tc/),
where Kraken ran [a celebrity Magic: The Gathering tournament](https://hub.b.tc/b26-mtg-tourney-presented-by-kraken)
and invited me. I have almost no MtG experience — every card in the
Old School Cube was new to me — and I still ended up across the
table from Gabriel Luna, Jimmy Wong, and Becky Lynch, people I'd
otherwise only see on a screen. At another table, Cassius Marsh
pulled a Black Lotus.

I didn't win. Had a lot of fun anyway.

More on the tournament itself in the next few days — there's a
longer post in there once I've sorted my notes.
