Mt. Gox grew from 3,000 users to become the world's largest Bitcoin
exchange, handling at its peak around 70% of all Bitcoin transactions
globally. The Bitcoin price went from roughly a dollar to over a thousand.
Coverage in Time, Forbes, and everywhere else. WikiLeaks started accepting
Bitcoin donations. Three years of building, firefighting, and trying to
keep up with something that was growing faster than anyone had planned for.
