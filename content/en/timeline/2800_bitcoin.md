About six months after founding Tibanne, a friend and client introduced
me to Bitcoin because of payment difficulties he was having from abroad.
I dug in immediately. The concept was extremely well designed—a
decentralised system where the mechanism itself was the trust layer.
I created the Bitcoin Wiki — Wikipedia was censoring cryptocurrency
content at the time, so I set up a dedicated MediaWiki instance that
became a central resource for the community and still runs today.
I mined coins in the evenings; one night I earned 50 BTC in a single
session. Tibanne became the first company in Japan to accept Bitcoin
payments.
