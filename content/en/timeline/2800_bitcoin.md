About six months after founding Tibanne, a friend and client introduced
me to Bitcoin because of payment difficulties he was having from abroad.
I dug in immediately. The concept was extremely well designed—a
decentralised system where the mechanism itself was the trust layer.
I contributed to the Bitcoin Wiki, mined coins in the evenings, and
Tibanne became the first company in Japan to accept Bitcoin payments.
