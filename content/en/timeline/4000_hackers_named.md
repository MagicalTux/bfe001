The US Department of Justice publicly named Alexey Bilyuchenko and
Aleksandr Verner — two Russian nationals accused of stealing 647,000 BTC
from Mt. Gox between September 2011 and May 2014. Bilyuchenko was also
charged as co-administrator of BTC-e alongside Vinnik. Kim Nilsson, who
had spent nearly a decade tracing the stolen coins, confirmed the
indictment aligned with his findings. After nine years, the thieves
had names.
