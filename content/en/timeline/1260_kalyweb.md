A hosting company I helped found in 2002, based in Lorient,
Bretagne. I built parts of the technical infrastructure and went on
site to rack servers myself. The company had its own dark fiber to
Paris — not the obvious choice for an outfit on the Atlantic coast,
but it gave us a real backbone. FF.ST came back up on Kalyweb.

The investors and the people actually building the thing had
different ideas about where the company should go. About a year
later the operators regrouped in Paris without them and started RYA
Network.
