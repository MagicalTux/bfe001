Mt. Gox filed for bankruptcy protection in Tokyo. At a hastily arranged
press conference, I acknowledged the disappearance of 850,000 BTC and
apologized. Nobuaki Kobayashi was appointed as bankruptcy trustee by the
Tokyo District Court. Two weeks later, my lawyers disclosed the existence
of 200,000 BTC I had stored separately in cold wallets. What followed
would take a decade to resolve.
