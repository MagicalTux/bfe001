Flew to Japan for the first time with Méko, a friend who had created
Pinpin Lelapin, Dating Sim, and Studio Tanuki. We were there to
represent MangaPop at the Tokyo Anime Fair. I stayed in a capsule hotel
and took hundreds of pictures. A brief trip, but enough to plant the
seed that would eventually pull me there for good.
