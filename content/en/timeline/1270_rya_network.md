The same operators who'd been at Kalyweb — minus the investors —
regrouped in Paris in 2003 and founded RYA Network with a different
set of backers. The name came from the three of us: Robert, Yann,
Antoine. Robert was my prénom d'usage at the time.

I worked on the infrastructure here too. Our hardware sponsor sent
us machines that were cheap and small but not strictly rackable, and
we crammed a lot of them into the datacenter anyway. I spent long
hours in the basement of the parent company, **L'@rt d'imprimer**,
a printing house in Paris.

Internal trouble sank it, and I ended up with nothing — an early
lesson in how hard it is to build something while still underage.
