My mother upgraded from the old 286/Macintosh to an actual modern PC—an
AMD K6-II running Windows 98. More importantly, we got ADSL with
Wanadoo and their weird green modem. After years of exploring machines in
isolation, the internet opened a whole new world. Everything I'd been doing
alone suddenly connected to everyone else doing the same thing.
