age: "MARCH 11, 2011"
machine: "東日本大震災"
spec: "Tōhoku earthquake, M9.1"
---

Ten days after taking over Mt. Gox. I was in Shibuya when the ground
started shaking. Sheltered in a nearby temple, helped strangers make phone
calls, then walked home across Tokyo. Tibane was safe. The French Embassy
organised evacuation flights, but my life was here. I stayed.
