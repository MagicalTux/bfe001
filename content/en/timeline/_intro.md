---
title: "Growing Up"
description: "Growing up between Dijon and Paris, from the ZX Spectrum to moving to Japan."
---

Born June 1, 1985 in Chenôve—the hospital for the Dijon area.
I grew up between Dijon and Paris, moved between my mother and my grandmother,
between schools that didn't quite know what to do with me. Each place came with
a different machine, and each machine taught me a different layer of how computing
works. By the time I was ten, I'd seen three levels of abstraction without
realising they had names.
