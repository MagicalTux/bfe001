On September 11, 2001, FF.ST lost its servers. It reopened in 2002
thanks to Kalyweb, then again in 2003 via RYA Network — two hosting
companies I helped found. RYA stood for Robert, Yann, Antoine (Robert
being my prénom d'usage). Internal trouble sank it, and I ended up with
nothing — an early lesson in how hard it is to build something while
still underage. When RYA's server shut down, the content was transferred
to Server4You, onto a machine called Kage (originally Arashi). FF.ST
kept going.
