CM1 at the Enseignement par Petit Groupes in Paris, living with my mother.
Her apartment had a 286 I wasn't allowed to touch, and later a Macintosh Plus.
HyperCard was a revelation—cards, stacks, buttons, scripts. An entirely
different way of thinking about software: event-driven, interactive, visual.
I didn't understand much HyperTalk (no documentation), but the experience of
building interactive systems stayed.
